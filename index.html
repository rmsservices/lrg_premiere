Why We Are Expanding

Our league continues to grow. Interest from new teams has increased, and we now have the ability to utilize both sides of the course effectively. In order to provide more opportunities for additional teams to join ‚Äî while preserving the culture that has made this league successful ‚Äî we are proposing an expansion beginning in 2026.

Expansion is the primary goal.
Creating two styles of competition is the secondary benefit.
By expanding to two divisions, we can:
  • Open additional team spots.
  • Reduce waitlists and turnover pressure.
  • Maintain the relaxed atmosphere many members value.
  • Provide a competitive pathway for teams seeking a higher-level format.
  • Align more closely with GHIN and USGA scoring standards so members can confidently post scores.

In recent seasons, questions have arisen regarding score posting because certain local rules prevented scores from being GHIN-compliant. By incorporating Maximum Net Double Bogey and aligning more closely with USGA structure in the Premiere League, players will be able to post scores properly, eliminating outside confusion and ensuring handicap integrity.

Unified Handicap System

Each player will carry one unified league handicap calculated using the lowest 4 of their most recent 6 league scores.

For players with fewer than 6 scores, 60% of available rounds (rounded up) will be used. Handicaps will be fully recalculated each week.

Rules Framework

  • USGA Rules apply.
  • Out of Bounds follows USGA Local Rule E-5 (two-stroke penalty, fairway drop).
  • Preferred Lies (Winter Rules) will be adopted in closely mown fairway areas.
  • Scores will remain GHIN-compliant and postable.

Grace & Pace Rule

  • Once a hole is mathematically decided, the losing player may pick up and proceed.
  • The player who picks up records a Maximum Net Double Bogey (GHIN/USGA definition).
  • A hole cannot be won by a Maximum Net Double Bogey; it must be won outright.
  • Maximum Net Double Bogey counts toward total net score for match points.
